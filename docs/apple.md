
VPN Protocol
---

TCP

```
------------------------Request------------------------

+---------+--------------+---------+-----------+------+
| IP TYPE | PAYLOAD TYPE | DEST IP | DEST PORT | IV   |
+---------+--------------+---------+-----------+------+
| 1       | 1            | 4 / 16  | 2         | 8-16 |
+---------+--------------+---------+-----------+------+

IP TYPE: IPv4, IPv6
PAYLOAD TYPE: TCP, UDP
DEST IP: Destination IP
DEST PORT: Destination Port
IV: Client Required IV

--------------------Response--------------------

+--------+--------+
| RESULT | REASON |
+--------+--------+
| 1      | 1      |
+--------+--------+

RESULT: Connection result. SUCCEED, FAILED
REASON: The reason of failure
```

UDP

```
--------------------------Request--------------------------

+---------+--------------+---------+-----------+----------+
| IP TYPE | PAYLOAD TYPE | DEST IP | DEST PORT | PAYLOAD  |
+---------+--------------+---------+-----------+----------+
| 1       | 1            | 4 / 16  | 2         | VARIABLE |
+---------+--------------+---------+-----------+----------+

--------------------Response--------------------

+------+----------+
| IV   | PAYLOAD  |
+------+----------+
| 8-16 | VARIABLE |
+------+----------+
```
